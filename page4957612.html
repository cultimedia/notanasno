Short-form Videos and Animations
Are the videos on your website turning visitors into clients?
They can be. Your website is the face of your business. It's the front door everyone walks through. It's one of the most valuable assets you own.

You already know that a great website conveys your company's vision, mission and value clearly. But are your videos doing the same?

Videos don't exist in a vacuum. They're part of a system-- a system with a purpose. In order to make really effective animations, it's necessary to know, really know, the brand that is being represented. What are the values? What is the brand identity? What is the brand voice?

These things have to be consistent at every touchpoint in order for your company to reap the benefits of a carefully considered brand.
